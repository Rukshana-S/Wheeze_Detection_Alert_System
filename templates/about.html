What is Wheeze?

Wheeze is a high-pitched whistling sound made while breathing, often indicating narrowed airways or respiratory conditions.

When to Seek Help

If wheeze is detected, consult a healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

Early Detection

Early identification of respiratory issues can lead to better treatment outcomes and improved quality of life.
